ステップ

How it works

Seven steps. All keyboard. Never leave Anki.

一

Watch Japanese YouTube

Open any Japanese video in Chrome. AJS runs silently in the background.

二

Hear something? Press the shortcut

Hit Ctrl+Shift+F the moment you hear an unfamiliar word.

三

Fuzzy-search the subtitle

A picker shows every subtitle segment — search in romaji, hiragana, or kanji. Three lines per entry.

四

Confirm your word

AJS extracts the target word and shows it to you. Press Enter to look it up.

五

AI builds the card

A local AI model (Ollama) generates the dictionary entry. Runs 100% offline.

六

Preview and approve

See the full card — kanji, reading, meaning, JLPT level, example sentences, audio.

七

Card added to Anki

Press Enter and the card lands in your deck. Done.

特徴

Features

🔒

100% Offline AI

Ollama runs locally on your machine. No API key. No subscription. No data leaves your computer.

🔊

Native Audio

Azure TTS generates natural Japanese audio (ja-JP-NanamiNeural) for every card you create.

🔍

Romaji / Kana / Kanji Search

Fuzzy-search subtitle segments however you think — romaji, hiragana, or kanji all work.

⌨️

Keyboard-First

Every step is Enter to confirm, Escape to go back. No mouse. No y/n prompts. No typing.

🔁

Auto Retry

Exponential backoff on LLM timeouts. Connection errors fail fast with a clear message.

🐛

Built-in Crash Reporter

If something breaks, one keypress files a full diagnostic report directly to GitHub.
